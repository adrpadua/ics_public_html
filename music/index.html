Adrian Roy

-music


I've been writing and recording my own music since I was a teenager.

I've played at venues like the Roxy and the House of Blues, and festivals like the Vans Warped Tour. Nowadays I'm writing & recording songs.

Enjoy!

Originals

All I Need

My first ever solo recording from my bedroom back in my parents house. Recorded it all with a single MXL990 condenser microphone and my guitar. I still enjoy listening to it from time to time.

Stay Right Here

A soft acoustic demo of a song I'm currently working on. It's about people you care about taking off for an extended trip to find themselves. Currently recorded a real studio version.

Le Paige

Hit the Lights EP

This was our debut EP for my old band, Le Paige. We raised money from a Kickstarter campaign to record this with Rich Mouser (Weezer, Chris Cornell, Oleander). I played guitar and sang backup vocals with the band.
